Attributes used:
age -> Age of the patient
sex -> Sex of the patient (1 = male, 0 = female)
cp -> Type of chest pain (1 - typical angina, 2 - atypical angina, 3 - non-anginal pain, 4 - asymptomatic pain)
trestbps -> resting blood pressure on admission to the Hospital
chol -> Cholestrohol
fbs -> Fasting blood sugar above 120 mg/dL (1 = true, 0 = false)
restecg -> Resting ECG results (0 = normal, 1 = ST-T wave abnormality, 2 = probable/definite left ventricular hypertrophy)
thalach -> maximum heart rate reached
exang -> exercise induced chest pain (1 = yes, 0 = no)
oldpeak -> ST depression induced by exercise realtive to rest
slope -> Slope of peak exercise ST segment
ca -> number of major vessels colored by fluorosopy
thal -> 1 = normal, 2 = fixed defect, 3 = reversable defect
target -> Diagnosis of heart disease (1 = increased risk of heart attack, 0 = decreased risk of heart attack)
